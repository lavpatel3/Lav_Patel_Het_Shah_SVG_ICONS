ZIMA

Since 1993

Zima or Zima clear malt is a carbonated beverage produced and distributed by the brewing company Coors. It is an adult beverage and comes in a variety of flavors. Zima first appeared on the market in 1993 as a beer substitute and was very popular at that time. However, in 2008, Zima ceased production in the United States. This has made Zima lovers feel very regretful. And there is only one place in the world where they can buy Zima.
Zima in Slav languages means “winter“. When it was released in the US it was called Zima clear malt. Zima is made from malt and has a fairly light alcohol content. In its first year on the market, Zima was very popular. Up to half of all drinkers in the US have tried Zima at least once.
